About

Our vision is an industry in which every individual, production and organisation has access to mental health and wellbeing support, where those who struggle with their mental health can share their experiences without fear of judgement or consequence, awareness and understanding of mental health issues is vastly improved, and the root causes of poor mental health in the workplace are addressed

What We Do

1

Improve awareness of mental health and wellbeing issues amongst those who work in the Arts

 

2

Supply ‘on the ground’ mental health and wellbeing support to TV and film sets, rehearsal rooms, studios, festivals, etc, and collaborate with organisations to build healthier and more creative working environments.

 

3

Provide individuals, productions and organisations with low-cost access to a diverse range of Counsellors, all of whom are BACP / UKCP registered, and have a knowledge, experience and understanding of the unique challenges of working in the Arts.

 

4

Deliver bespoke mental health and wellbeing workshops, training courses, and surgeries.

 

5

Campaign for positive change within the industry and challenge the root causes of poor mental health in the workplace.
